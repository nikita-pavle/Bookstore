The Hunger Games

A dystopian novel by Suzanne Collins,set in a post-apocalyptic world where teenagers are forced to fight to the death in an annual event called the Hunger Games.

The Hunger Games is a young-adult dystopian novel by Suzanne Collins. The story is set in the country of Panem, which consists of the wealthy Capitol and 13 districts in varying states of poverty. Every year, children from the first 12 districts are selected via lottery to participate in a compulsory televised battle royale death match called The Hunger Games. The story follows Katniss Everdeen, a 16-year-old girl who offers herself as a tribute in the annual Hunger Games in the post-apocalyptic world of Panem
